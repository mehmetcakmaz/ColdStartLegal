Privacy Policy

Effective July 5, 2026

No data collected

ColdStart does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs in the app.

No account required

ColdStart has no sign-up, no login, and no backend servers. Your alarms, labels, and sound choices are stored only on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

Alarms

Alarms are scheduled and delivered by Apple's AlarmKit framework on your device. ColdStart asks for alarm permission solely to ring at the times you choose.

Purchases

The optional one-time purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. ColdStart never sees or handles your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for how Apple processes purchases.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
